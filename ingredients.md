For making a pizza the required ingredients are:
1) Onion (1/4 cup)
2) Capsicum (2 Tbsp)
3) Tomato (1/4 cup)
4) Oregano (1/4 tsp)
5) Olives
6) Boiled corn (1 Tbsp)
7) Pepper (1/4 tsp)
8) Salt (1/2 tsp)
9) Maida flour (1 cup)
10) Corn Flour (1 Tbsp)
11) Baking Soda (1/2 tsp)
12) Baking Powder (1/2 tsp)
13) Curd (1/4 cup)
14) Oil (1 Tbsp)
15) Water
16) Cheese
17) Tomato sauce
